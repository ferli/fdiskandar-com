## Domain Expertise Areas

### 1. Digital Transformation (Transformasi Digital)

**Focus Areas:**
- Modernizing legacy systems to digital-first operations
- ERP implementation and system integration
- Business process redesign for operational efficiency
- Organizational change management for technology adoption
- Cloud infrastructure and scalability

**Common Challenges Addressed:**
- Isolated legacy systems that don't integrate
- Data scattered across systems with no single source of truth
- Manual, inefficient processes prone to errors
- Limited IT capacity and expertise
- Budget constraints versus infrastructure needs
- Organizational resistance to change

**Solutions Framework:**
- Maturity assessment of current state
- Business case development with clear ROI
- Phased, prioritized roadmaps (quick wins → core transformation)
- Technology selection based on sustainability
- Change management programs
- Continuous improvement monitoring

### 2. Governance, Risk & Compliance (Tata Kelola, Risiko & Kepatuhan)

**Focus Areas:**

- Risk identification, assessment, and mitigation
- Compliance with regulatory requirements
- Vendor management and due diligence

- Cybersecurity governance for critical infrastructure

**Indonesian Regulatory Context:**

- Audit BPK/BPKP mechanisms
- BSSN critical infrastructure guidelines
- Regional government policies
- BPJS compliance

**Governance Components:**
1. Clear organizational structure and accountability
2. Risk management processes
3. Compliance adherence mechanisms
4. Internal and detective controls

### 3. Non-Revenue Water Reduction (Pengurangan Air Hilang)

**Focus Areas:**
- Physical water loss (kebocoran/leaks) reduction
- Commercial loss (kehilangan komersial) elimination
- Pressure management and leak detection
- Meter management and smart metering
- NRW monitoring systems
- Operational excellence for field teams

**Impact Metrics:**
- Typical Indonesian PDAM NRW: 30-40%
- Financial impact: 30% NRW = 30% potential revenue loss
- Operational cost reduction through efficiency
- Environmental sustainability through reduced extraction

**Implementation Strategy:**
- Accurate NRW assessment and baseline
- Pressure management systems (PRV)
- Active leak detection programs
- Meter replacement and smart metering
- Real-time monitoring dashboards
- Operational procedures and training

**Technology Enablers:**
- Automated Pressure Reducing Valves (PRV)
- Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI)
- SCADA systems
- Geographic Information Systems (GIS)
- AI/ML analytics for predictive maintenance

### 4. Cybersecurity & Critical Infrastructure Protection

**Focus Areas:**
- SCADA system security
- Critical infrastructure protection
- Customer data protection
- Incident response and recovery
- Security governance frameworks
- Compliance with BSSN guidelines
