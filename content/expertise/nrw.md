## Non-Revenue Water (NRW) Reduction Strategy

Non-Revenue Water adalah air yang diproduksi namun tidak menghasilkan revenue karena kebocoran (physical loss) atau kehilangan komersial (commercial loss). Untuk PDAM Indonesia rata-rata, NRW mencapai 30-40%—peluang besar untuk improvement.

### The Challenge

NRW bukan hanya masalah finansial:

- **Revenue Loss** — 30% NRW = 30% revenue loss untuk utilitas yang sama
- **Operational Inefficiency** — Meningkatkan production cost untuk memenuhi demand
- **Sustainability** — Excessive water extraction dari sumber daya yang terbatas
- **Customer Satisfaction** — Frequent outages akibat production insufficient

### Root Cause Analysis

NRW berasal dari dua kategori:

**Physical Loss (Kebocoran):**
- Aged infrastructure & corrosion
- Pressure management inefficiency
- Leaks di main, secondary, service lines

**Commercial Loss (Kehilangan Komersial):**
- Meter inaccuracy atau non-functioning meters
- Illegal connections
- Meter reading errors
- Billing inefficiency

### Integrated Reduction Strategy

Effective NRW reduction memerlukan comprehensive approach:

1. **Assessment & Baseline** — Accurate NRW calculation dan component analysis
2. **Pressure Management** — Optimize system pressure untuk reduce leakage
3. **Leak Detection** — Active leakage locating & repair prioritization
4. **Meter Management** — Meter replacement program & calibration
5. **Non-Revenue Water Management System** — Monitoring & reporting infrastructure
6. **Operational Excellence** — Training & procedural improvements

### Technology Enablers

- **Pressure Reducing Valves (PRV)** — Automated pressure optimization
- **Advanced Metering** — IoT sensors & smart meters untuk accurate data
- **SCADA Systems** — Real-time monitoring & alerting
- **GIS Integration** — Network mapping untuk leak localization
- **AI/ML** — Predictive analytics untuk leak identification

### Expected Outcomes

Dengan implementasi sistematis:

- **Year 1:** 3-5% NRW reduction
- **Year 2:** 5-8% cumulative reduction
- **Year 3+:** Sustained improvement dengan maintenance excellence
